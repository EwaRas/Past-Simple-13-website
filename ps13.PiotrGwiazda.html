past simple 13

Piotr Gwiazda

KIDS AT THE ZOO


Keep your distance
                        or we’ll spit on you.

WIND TURBINES


                        They look gentle from a distance
                        but up close
                        they become menacing,
                        sword-wielding samurai.

                        You have lost everything
                        and I love my life.

THE PALM OF YOUR HAND


                        Nothing to see:
                        just crevices,
                        fissures,
                        empty riverbeds…

HORRIFIC ADVENTURES


                        I waded
                        through seaweed and kelp.

                        You took some pictures
                        of caves.

Piotr Gwiazda's

most recent books are U.S. Poetry in the Age of Empire (Palgrave Macmillan, 2014) and Aspects of Strangers (Moria Books, 2015). His translation of Grzegorz Wróblewski’s new poems, Zero Visibility, was published by Phoneme Media in 2017. He teaches at the University of Pittsburgh.
